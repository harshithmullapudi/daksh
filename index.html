Daksh

SASTRA University proudly presents the second largest Techno-Management Fest in South India with grandeur and magnificence. Bringing out nurturing talent and flair, it is one stop for all that is exhilarating. Clustering different spheres of technology into one nutshell, Daksh offers an all-round experience in one cup of tea. From Technical Events to sharpen your prowess, to out of the box Techno-Fun events, Daksh keeps molding dexterity. Innovation and Versatility go hand in hand as once again as the Fest returns once again for its next edition with its magnanimity unturned.

OutReach

  • Spardha

    Aimed at bringing intellects together, Spardha sets a right platform for budding quizzers to stand out. With over 100 teams across various cities in south India, dueling in the battle of wits, Spardha sparks the right start for inquisitive minds.

  • Spectra

    Aspiring to discover the tech-savvy you, blaze the trail at Spectra! With a range of mind bewildering events at your disposal, let your curiosity kill the cat as you strike the hill top! Keep your spirits up and stay tuned!

  • H

    Hackathon

    Moving up the ladder, Hackathon contest is all set to challenge the tech aficionados! Get set to jump in to the first ever battle at such a grand scale! Warm up as the fixture is all set to begin!

  • Gaming Events

    Call of duty, DOTA, FIFA, Mini Militia and what not? Bring out the dauntless gamer inside your combatant souls, because we are going to swoop down to bring out the belligerent you!

  • Robo Wars

    Fascinated and ready to zoom up for an exciting race? Make four-wheeled wonders that howls like a hunk, wails vigorously and moves majestically! Feel the adrenaline-rush as your car steals the limelight! Can your dream car race up front to the finish line first?
